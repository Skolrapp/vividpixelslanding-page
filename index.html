Questions

Questions couples often ask.

Simple answers before you begin your inquiry.

Do we need to know how to pose?

No. You do not need to arrive knowing what to do. We guide gently when needed and give space when the moment is naturally unfolding.

How early should we book?

As soon as your date is confirmed. We accept a limited number of weddings so each celebration receives proper attention, planning, and care.

Do you cover weddings outside Dar es Salaam?

Yes. We cover weddings in Dar es Salaam and selected destinations across Tanzania. Travel requirements can be discussed during inquiry.

When will we receive our photos and films?

Delivery timelines depend on the type of coverage selected. We communicate expected timelines clearly before confirmation so you know what to expect.

Can we customize a collection?

Yes. Our collections provide a starting point, but coverage can be refined depending on your wedding size, location, and priorities.

Will the day feel overly directed?

No. Our approach is calm and intentional. We step in when guidance helps, but we avoid turning real moments into forced scenes.
